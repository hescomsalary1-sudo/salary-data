Grievance Workflow

01

Submit Request

Fill out the digital form with your details, category of grievance, and a clear description of the issue.

02

Initial Review

Your ticket is automatically assigned to the relevant department head for initial verification.

03

Processing

The authority investigates the matter and provides remarks or forwards it to the necessary official.

04

Resolution

Once fixed, the status changes to "Resolved." You can then view the final outcome in your Track Status page.

!

Appeal Option

If you are not satisfied with the resolution, you have 7 days to file an appeal for higher authority review.
